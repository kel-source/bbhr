About Us

BBHR is a skater owned and run organisation. Born on the 4th July 2011, we are a team of like minded skaters, referees, NSO's and volunteers.

Fancy Giving it a Go?

New skaters (male and female) are always welcome. We teach you how to skate, how to fall and how to play the awesome sport that is Roller Derby! We have regular rookie "taster sessions" so keep an eye on our web page calendar for upcoming dates.
